AI Ops Hub is deployed.

1. Get service endpoint:
   kubectl get svc {{ include "aiops-hub.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. Port-forward locally:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "aiops-hub.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

3. Verify health:
   curl http://127.0.0.1:8080/health
